The Atlas V Rocket Launch:

OSIRIS-REx Mission

The OSIRIS-REx mission is launching to go to a near-Earth asteroid called Bennu. The mission is to bring back a small sample of said asteroid for study. Launched from Cape Canaveral Air Force base and will be in space for seven years. It will be approaching the asteroid in 2018, to survey for a landing site and will then bring back the samples if landing is successful.

Some fun facts:

  • launched September 8, 2016
  • will arrive August 17, 2018
  • will take sample July 4, 2020
  • will leave Bennu March 3, 2021
  • will land on Earth September 24, 2023

Update: the Atlas V successfully launched!
